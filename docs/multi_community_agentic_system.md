## 🎼 Protognosis-Style Supervisor

### **🧠 Advanced Optimization Engine**
- **Combinatorial Analysis**: Evaluates up to 50 mutation combinations per iteration
- **Synergy Detection**: Identifies beneficial mutation interactions
- **Community Weighting**: Dynamic performance-based community weights
- **Diversity Optimization**: Ensures diverse, robust mutation selections

### **📊 Multi-Criteria Decision Making**
- **Individual Scores**: Stability effect × Confidence × Community weight
- **Synergy Analysis**: Pairwise interaction scoring with distance decay
- **Diversity Bonuses**: Community diversity (15%) + Expert diversity (10%)
- **Consensus Weighting**: Cross-community agreement bonus (25%)

### **🔬 Selection Algorithm**
1. **Proposal Collection**: Gather all community proposals
2. **Deduplication**: Remove duplicate positions, keep highest confidence
3. **Interaction Matrix**: Calculate pairwise synergy scores
4. **Combination Generation**: Create diverse candidate combinations
5. **Multi-Criteria Evaluation**: Score each combination holistically
6. **Optimal Selection**: Choose highest-scoring combination
